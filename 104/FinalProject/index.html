My Journey

A little about my educational journey: during high school, I applied to colleges under a different major and only got into my safety schools. However, after getting rejected by USC, I was given the opportunity to transfer through the Trojan Transfer Program (TTP). This program gave me the opportunity to talk with a USC advisor to learn about what I could do to increase my chances of transfer. Instead of going to my safety schools, I decided to go to Irvine Valley College and transfer into USC as a sophomore transfer.
